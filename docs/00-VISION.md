# Project Vision

## Mission

Build the most trusted Trading Intelligence Platform for retail traders.

The platform exists to help traders make better decisions using structured market intelligence instead of hype or prediction.

---

## Vision Statement

Create a platform where:

Trading generates Research.

Research generates Content.

Content builds Trust.

Trust builds Community.

Community supports Software, Products and Education.

---

## Core Values

### Trust

Everything we publish should be transparent and verifiable.

### Simplicity

Complex market information should become easy to understand.

### Consistency

Small improvements every trading day.

### Education

Teach people how to think.

Not what to buy.

### Long-term Thinking

Every feature should still make sense five years from now.

---

## North Star

Become the first website serious retail traders visit before every trading session.

---

## Success Metrics

Primary

- Daily Returning Users

Secondary

- Newsletter Subscribers
- Journal Readers
- Research Readers
- Premium Members

Never optimize only for page views.
